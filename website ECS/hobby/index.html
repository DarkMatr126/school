Video Games

Assassin's Creed Mirage was my first true AC game. It has great stealth and parkour mechanics, and it was really fun to run around 9th century Bagdad.

Assassin's Creed Unity plays in Paris during the french revolution. You play as a street rat turned assassin, trying to prevent the revolution from going overboard, and avenging your dead father figures.

Jedi Survivor is one of my favorite games of all time. It is set in the Star Wars Universe, and follows a Jedi name Cal Kestis. It has a decent story, and absolutely insane combat mechanics.
